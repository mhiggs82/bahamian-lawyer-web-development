About Nathaniel R. Bethel

Two Decades of Property Law Across the Islands

Called to the Bahamas Bar in 2003, Nathaniel R. Bethel has dedicated his career to the single area of law most fundamental to Bahamian life and commerce: real property. From his chambers on East Bay Street in Nassau, he advises local families, international purchasers, resort developers, and institutional investors on the full lifecycle of property transactions.

Before establishing his own practice, Nathaniel spent seven years at a leading Nassau commercial firm, where he led the property and conveyancing department. He holds an LL.B. from the University of the West Indies and a postgraduate diploma in Land Economy from the University of Cambridge. He is a member of the Bahamas Bar Association and a frequent contributor to the Bahamas Real Estate Association's legal advisory committee.

Areas of Expertise

Real Estate & Property Practice Areas

End-to-end management of property transfers, title searches, title insurance, and registration under the Registered Land Act. I ensure clean title delivery on every transaction, from single-family homes to multi-parcel commercial assemblages.

  • Title searches and opinion letters
  • Purchase and sale agreements
  • Mortgage documentation
  • Stamp duty and VAT compliance

Advising developers from land acquisition through project completion. I structure joint ventures, negotiate construction contracts, secure government approvals, and ensure regulatory compliance under the Planning and Subdivisions Act.

  • Subdivision approvals and zoning
  • Construction and architect contracts
  • Joint venture structuring
  • Environmental impact compliance

Guiding international buyers and investors through the Bahamas Investments Board approval process, International Persons Landholding Act requirements, and cross-border structuring for residential and commercial acquisitions.

  • Investment Board applications
  • International Persons Landholding Act compliance
  • Cross-border tax structuring
  • Permanent residency through property ownership

Resolving boundary disputes, adverse possession claims, easement conflicts, and title challenges through negotiation, mediation, or Supreme Court litigation. I protect your property rights with thorough preparation and measured advocacy.

  • Boundary and survey disputes
  • Adverse possession claims
  • Specific performance actions
  • Quieting of title

Drafting and negotiating commercial leases, ground leases, and lease-to-own arrangements for retail, office, hospitality, and industrial properties across Nassau, Grand Bahama, and the Family Islands.

  • Commercial lease negotiation
  • Ground lease structuring
  • Tenant and landlord advisory
  • Lease enforcement and disputes

Advising developers and owners' corporations on strata plan registration, by-law drafting, common property management, and unit sales under the Law of Property and Conveyancing (Condominium) Act.

  • Strata plan registration
  • Corporation by-laws and governance
  • Common property disputes
  • Unit sale documentation
