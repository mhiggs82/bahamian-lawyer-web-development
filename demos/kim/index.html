About

Trusted Counsel for Complex Transactions

Kimberley A. Rolle is a Partner at McKinney, Bancroft & Hughes, one of The Bahamas' most distinguished law firms. She serves as Chair of four of the firm's practice groups — Real Estate, Foreign and Local Investment, Immigration, and Corporate & Commercial — reflecting the breadth and depth of her expertise.

Called to the Bar in both England & Wales and The Bahamas in 2005, Kimberley brings over two decades of sophisticated legal counsel to individuals, corporations, and financial institutions operating in and across The Bahamas. Her practice spans the full spectrum of commercial law, with particular strength in high-value real estate transactions, cross-border corporate structuring, banking, and project finance.

Beyond private practice, Kimberley serves as a Director of the Securities Commission of The Bahamas and holds membership on the Disciplinary Committee of the Bahamas Real Estate Association — positions that underscore her standing as both a practitioner and a steward of the legal profession.
