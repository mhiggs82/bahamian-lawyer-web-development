Professional Profile

Partner, Advocate,
Public Servant

Michela E. Barnett-Ellis is a Partner in the Dispute Resolution practice at Graham Thompson — one of The Bahamas' most respected commercial law firms. With nearly two decades of legal practice, she brings rigorous analytical skill and a results-driven approach to every matter she handles.

Her practice focuses primarily on asset and debt recovery for large domestic and international banks, security enforcement in the real estate and banking sectors, and contentious commercial and land disputes. She also advises extensively on family law, including divorce, matrimonial disputes, adoptions, and child-related proceedings.

Beyond her legal career, Michela was appointed to the Senate (Upper House) of The Bahamas in February 2022 — a role that reflects her commitment to public service and her advocacy for equality and representation. She is also the Free National Movement (FNM) candidate for the Killarney constituency in the 2026 General Election.

A mother of a young child and a multi-generational community advocate, Michela is known for her grounded, direct approach — and her unwavering belief that effective legal counsel and genuine public service go hand in hand.
