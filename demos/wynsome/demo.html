About

A Practice Built on
Excellence & Trust

Wynsome D. Carey is a Partner at Alexiou Knowles & Co., one of The Bahamas' most respected legal institutions. She serves in both the Property and Development Practice Group and the Litigation and Dispute Resolution Practice Group, bringing a rare breadth of expertise to each client engagement.

With a dual academic foundation in Economics and Law, Wynsome brings a commercially astute perspective to complex disputes, real estate transactions, and international financial matters. Her practice spans civil and commercial litigation, real property, banking and finance, family law, and admiralty — enabling her to serve clients across a wide spectrum of legal needs.

She recently appeared as Junior Counsel before the Supreme Court in a landmark multi-jurisdictional trust dispute, addressing the jurisdictional limits of Bahamian courts in trust-related arbitrations.
