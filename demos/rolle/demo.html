About Cedric A. Rolle

Charting a Course Through Complex Maritime Law

Called to the Bahamas Bar in 2004, Cedric A. Rolle has built a distinguished practice focused exclusively on maritime and admiralty law. From the historic chambers on Bay Street in Nassau, he advises vessel owners, commercial shipping lines, marine insurers, port operators, and individual seafarers on the full spectrum of maritime legal matters.

After earning his LL.B. from the University of the West Indies and completing advanced maritime law studies at the University of London (IMO International Maritime Law Institute), Cedric returned to Nassau with a clear mission: to provide Bahamian and international clients with world-class maritime legal counsel rooted in deep local knowledge.
