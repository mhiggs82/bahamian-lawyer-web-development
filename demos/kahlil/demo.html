Biography

A Career Defined by Precedent and Principle

Kahlil D. Parker KC is a Partner and the Head of the Litigation Practice at Cedric L. Parker & Co., one of The Bahamas' most established law firms. Called to the Bahamas Bar and the Bar of England & Wales in 2005, he took Silk in 2021, becoming a King's Counsel — recognition of the highest distinction in the Commonwealth legal tradition.

Mr. Parker is a highly skilled appellate lawyer with a practice that has involved precedent-setting cases establishing Bahamian law on significant areas of trust law, company law, public law, industrial relations, and bankruptcy law — before the Supreme Court, the Court of Appeal, and the Privy Council.

He serves as Counsel for many Financial Institutions, Trade Unions, and Private Companies in The Bahamas, and provides corporate, commercial, regulatory, and industrial relations consultancy alongside his active litigation practice.

The Firm
