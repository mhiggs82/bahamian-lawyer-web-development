About Simone V. Cartwright

A Trusted Voice in Bahamian Financial Law

Simone V. Cartwright was called to the Bahamas Bar in 2007 and has since built one of Nassau's most respected financial law practices. She advises domestic and international clients on the full range of regulatory, transactional, and compliance matters that arise under the Securities Industry Act, the Investment Funds Act, and the Banks and Trust Companies Regulation Act.

Before establishing her own chambers, Simone served as Associate Counsel at the Securities Commission of The Bahamas, where she gained firsthand insight into the regulatory landscape that now informs every aspect of her practice. She holds an LL.B. from the University of the West Indies and an LL.M. in International Banking and Finance Law from University College London.

How I Work

01

Regulatory Assessment

A thorough review of your business model, licensing requirements, and exposure to Bahamian financial regulations. I identify obligations, gaps, and opportunities before any action is taken.

Strategic Structuring

Developing compliant structures that serve your commercial goals. Whether launching a fund, obtaining a licence, or reorganising a trust, every solution is built around your specific circumstances.
