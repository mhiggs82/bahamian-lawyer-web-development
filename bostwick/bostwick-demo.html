About Our Firm

A Legacy of Legal Excellence in Nassau

For over three decades, Bostwick, Bostwick & Co has been a pillar of legal excellence in The Bahamas. Located in the heart of Nassau on historic George Street, our firm has built an unparalleled reputation for integrity, expertise, and client-focused service.

Our team of experienced attorneys brings deep knowledge of Bahamian law across a comprehensive range of practice areas, from criminal defense to corporate matters, family law to immigration services.
